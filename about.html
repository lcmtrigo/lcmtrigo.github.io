What are your strongest qualities?

I'm organized and well rounded. My approach towards design challenges is simplistic and logical, having a thought-out plan focusing on the journey in between the start and destination

What made you want be a product designer?

I wanted a career that combines my technical knowledge with my creativity. I wanted to help people with visuals and see the impact of my work as end-users interact with those visuals. Product design is essentially what I was looking for, summated into one role.

Where did you go to school?

I went to BCIT and graduated from the New Media Design and Web Development program with distinction, but my learning didn't stop there. I'm a lifelong learner who stays updated on trends and learns skills that might prove useful someday.

Where do you see yourself in 5-10 years?

I see myself as a product or interaction designer at an agency or startup at the senior level. I would also help junior designers with improving their skills and getting a job in the tech industry.

What do you do in your spare time?

I listen to and play music (I'm a self-taught musician), make vector art with Illustrator, watch movies, play video games, work out, enjoy clothes and hang out at coffee shops.

What's an interesting fact about you?

You know that blue bag of Munchies that has four different kinds of chips (I think it was Cheetos, Doritos, Sun Chips and Pretzels)? I actually like the pretzels.
