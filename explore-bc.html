Building a travel and tourism website with Bootstrap at Explore BC

Web Development

Time frame:April 2017 to May 2017
Tools used: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Adobe Creative Suite

Explore BC is a marketing website aimed at encouraging BC denizens to go out explore the natural wonders of the province. It informs the reader with various suggested activities and where to go to purchase tools and equipment. The site also encourages social media participation.

Bootstrap was the principal front-end framework used to make this website. I was able to form the content to my will by reading the documentation, handpicking the necessary classes for this project and visually customizing them with custom CSS. I also used Photoshop to optimize the images for the web.
